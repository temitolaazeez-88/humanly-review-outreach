Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Humanly Review Outreach may collect from clients and how we use it to provide professional outreach services.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, company name, website, campaign details, target audience, budget range, product or service information, and other details needed to review your campaign.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to review your campaign, communicate with you, prepare outreach plans, research suitable creators, provide reporting, deliver services, and improve our internal workflow.

3. Creator Research

Creator due diligence is based on publicly available information, client-provided information, creator-provided information, and professional assessment. We do not use hacking, unauthorized private data, or private account access.

4. Confidentiality

We treat client campaign details, outreach lists, strategy notes, and internal reports as confidential unless you authorize sharing or disclosure is required by law.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell client information. We may share campaign details with creators or service providers only when needed to support an approved campaign, communication, or service delivery.

6. Email Communication

By contacting us, you agree that we may reply by email about your inquiry, project, proposal, or service request.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable care to protect client information. However, no online communication or storage method is completely secure.

8. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at outreach@humanlyreview.com.
